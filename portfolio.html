My Projects

Mini-project - Electricity Billing Management System

For this mini-project, I developed an Electricity Billing Management System using SQL. The system automates the entire billing process by tracking monthly electricity consumption and calculating the cost per unit of electricity. I utilized key DBMS techniques including DDL (Data Definition Language) for structuring the database, DML (Data Manipulation Language) for data retrieval and modification, DCL (Data Control Language) for managing access controls, and TCL (Transaction Control Language) to ensure data consistency and integrity. This project efficiently manages data flow, ensuring accurate billing and streamlined operations.
